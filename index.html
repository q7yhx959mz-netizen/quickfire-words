QUICKFIRE WORDS

Fast paced word chaos for teams.

HOW TO PLAY

Describe the word without using any banned words.


Your team must guess before the timer ends.


Correct guesses earn points.


The final turn is a BONUS ROUND where you choose from 4 words with different point values.


Saying a banned word in the bonus round subtracts points.


PAUSE BUTTON: Pause and resume the timer anytime.


ADD TO HOME SCREEN:
